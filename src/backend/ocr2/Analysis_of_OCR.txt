Problems:

headers repeated every page
noise like lab address
some broken spacing
tables distorted


Design principles to be implemented:

Whitelist important things (measurements, test names, values, units, reference ranges). Keep anything matching these.
Blacklist obvious noise (headers, footers, page numbers, lab addresses, boilerplate). Remove anything matching these.
Detect repeated lines across pages (usually headers/footers) and drop them.
Canonicalize and normalize (units, spacing, date formats).
Fallback human-in-loop for new formats + logging so patterns are discoverable.
Test + metrics (precision/recall for measurement extraction) and small sample QA.
