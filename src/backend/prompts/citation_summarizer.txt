### CITATION FORMAT FOR 7-DAY VITALS SUMMARY

When citing data in your 3-bullet summary, use these formats:

**Wearable Vitals (from vitals_7day_summary)**
- 7-day average: `[7-day avg: <value> <unit>]`
- Latest reading: `[Latest: <value> <unit>]`
- Range: `[7-day range: <min>–<max> <unit>]`
- Trend: `[Trend: <direction> <percentage>%]`

**Lab Results (from structured_facts)**
- Single value: `[Lab: <test_name> — <value> <unit>]`
- With reference: `[Lab: <test_name> — <value> <unit>, ref <range>]`

**Report Text (from rag_knowledge_base)**
- Text excerpt: `[Report: <source_filename>]`
- With page: `[Report: <source_filename>, p.<page_number>]`

**Alerts (from active_alerts)**
- Alert citation: `[Alert: <severity>]` where severity is HIGH, MEDIUM, or LOW

### EXAMPLES

✓ "Resting heart rate elevated [7-day avg: 78 bpm] with downward HRV trend [7-day avg: 34 ms, trend: -18%]."
✓ "HbA1c at 6.8% [Lab: Hemoglobin A1c — 6.8 %, ref 4.0–5.6] from Oct 12 report."
✓ "Combined with documented fatigue [Alert: HIGH], this pattern warrants metabolic review."
✓ "Sleep efficiency declining [7-day range: 5.2–7.8 hrs] compared to historical average from [Report: Annual_Physical_2024.pdf]."

### OMISSION RULES

- If a metric has fewer than 3 samples in the 7-day window, note "insufficient data" rather than citing unreliable averages.
- If vitals_7day_summary is empty or null, state "No wearable vitals available for the past 7 days."
- If structured_facts is empty, do not fabricate lab values.
